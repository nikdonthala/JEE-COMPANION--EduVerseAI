# JEE-COMPANION--EduVerseAI 
Our aim is to create the MVP of an AI-powered student application, starting with a JEE Mentor AI called EduVerseAI. Instead of students switching between YouTube, Google, ChatGPT, coaching apps, and planners, our platform brings everything together in one place. 

The MVP includes an AI JEE mentor, an AI timetable generator, a study planner, and a progress dashboard. In the future, this platform will expand into a Mega AI Companion with specialized mentors for Boards, BITSAT, NEET, coding, college counselling, and career guidance.
